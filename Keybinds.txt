Press I to make a ground farmable/undo also
Press Space to attack
Press P to place blocks
Press 1-9 to change inventory slot
Press E to open crafting menu
Mouse press on the inventory and crafting menu to drag and drop things where they need to be (Left click all items, right click half of the items)
To put a seed on farmable ground, just select the slot the seed is located in and right click
Mouse press when a plant is fully grown to harvest it's contents
